I work as Post-Doctoral Researcher at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou, China. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning with a focus on object recognition, video understanding,and brain inspired computational model. More recently, I am also interested in more higher-level cognitive tasks (e.g., abstract visual reasoning).

Before joining the Sun Yat-sen University, I received my Ph.D. degree from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2020 (March) on computer vision and machine learning.
