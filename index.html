I am an Associate Professor at Sun Yat-sen University. My research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, AI4Neuroscience and Neuroscience4AI. Previously, I work as Post-Doctoral Researcher at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou, China. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning with a focus on object recognition, video understanding,and brain inspired computational model. More recently, I am also interested in more higher-level cognitive tasks (e.g., abstract visual reasoning).
